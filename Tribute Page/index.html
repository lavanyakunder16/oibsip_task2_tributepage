WHO IS SUNDAR PICHAI ?


  • Sundar Pichai was born on July 12, 1972 in Chennai, India.
  • He holds a degree in Metallurgical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.
  • He was appointed as the CEO of Google in 2015, following the creation of Alphabet Inc.
  • Pichai also serves as the CEO of Alphabet Inc. since December 2019, following the departure of Larry Page.
  • He is known for his advocacy of artificial intelligence and its potential to solve some of the world's biggest problems.

    • QUOTES !

    One of Pichai's most famous quotes is "You have to be comfortable with the notion that in order to build something great, you have to be focused and picky." This quote speaks to Pichai's philosophy of focusing on a few key things and doing them exceptionally well. In order to truly innovate, Pichai believes that you have to be willing to say no to many things and stay focused on your core mission

    Pichai has also been inspired by his upbringing in India, where he witnessed firsthand the transformative power of technology. In an interview with The New York Times, he spoke about how his father, who worked as an electrical engineer for the British conglomerate GEC, would bring home technology magazines and talk to him about the latest developments in the field. This early exposure to technology inspired Pichai to pursue a career in the field himself.

    Throughout his career, Pichai has remained committed to his mission of using technology to make the world a better place. He has spoken about the importance of building products that are accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or economic status. Under his leadership, Google has launched several initiatives aimed at bringing the benefits of technology to underserved communities, such as its Digital Skills for Africa program.
